# HazardXR — Safety Simulation for Quest 3

Portfolio site for HazardXR, a modular MR/VR safety simulation series built for Meta Quest 3.

**Live site:** https://amukiria.github.io

---

## About

HazardXR is designed and built by Albert Mukiria — Senior Product Manager and XR developer with hands-on Unity 6 and Quest 3 capability. The series demonstrates what becomes possible when product strategy meets immersive technology in high-stakes training contexts.

Each episode is a standalone scenario built from a documented safety failure. The shell handles session management, progression, and scoring. Episodes load on top of it as self-contained modules. No episode starts until the one before it ships.

| Episode | Title | Domain | Standard |
|---|---|---|---|
| E01 | Fire | Fire safety · Industrial environment | NFPA 10 · OSHA 1910.157 |

| E04 | Hazard Identification | General industry · Risk awareness | ISO 31000 · ISO 45001 |
| E05 | Line of Fire | Struck-by / caught-in hazards | ISO 45001 Cl. 6.1 · BBS |

| E07 | Chemical Handling | Hazmat · Toxic substance exposure | GHS · OSHA 1910.1200 |
| E08 | Respiratory Protection | PPE selection · Hazardous atmospheres | ISO 16972 · OSHA 1910.134 |

E01 is the active build. All subsequent episodes are planned in sequence.

---

## Standards Approach

Episodes reference ISO standards as the primary global framework, with OSHA as a secondary US-market reference where applicable. Behavioural episodes (E10) reference industry frameworks (IOGP). This approach reflects the geography-agnostic positioning of the series.
