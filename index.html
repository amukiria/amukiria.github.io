THE SERIES

Seven episodes. Seven failures that cannot happen twice.

Each episode is a standalone scenario built from a documented safety failure. The shell handles session management, progression, and scoring. Episodes load on top of it as self-contained modules. No episode starts until the one before it ships.

ABOUT

Built by a product leader

HazardXR is designed and built by Albert Mukiria — Senior Product Manager and XR developer with hands-on Unity and Quest 3 capability.

This series demonstrates what becomes possible when product strategy meets immersive technology in high-stakes training contexts. The build order is deliberate: shell first, then E01, then subsequent episodes in sequence. No episode starts until the one before it ships.

The goal is not novelty. The goal is behaviour change at the moment that matters.
